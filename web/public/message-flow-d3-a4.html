Closed Loop (A4)

A submitter is both a producer and a subscriber. The pipeline is a closed loop: an Actor produces to the Queue → Workers (the queue consumers / AKS clusters) drain & execute → publish to the Topic → the completion is delivered back to the same Actor (the teal loop over the top). So svc-batch appears once, on the left, but its arrows go out (produce) and come back in (subscribe). Mock data
